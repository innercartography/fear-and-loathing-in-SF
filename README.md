## What This Is

On March 4, 2026, Vitalik Buterin and Beff Jezos (Guillaume Verdon) squared off at Frontier Tower in San Francisco — e/acc vs d/acc, the great thermodynamic debate about whether to floor it toward computational godhood or pump the differential brakes.

Neither framework asked the obvious question: **who's not on the rocket?**

This is a counter-argument. Not against acceleration. Against the selective blindness that treats 20,000+ people cycling through San Francisco's homeless services every year as someone else's variable.

The argument is simple:

- We have enough tech. We lack wisdom, coordination, and care.
- The people who've fallen off the edge have superior pattern recognition — forged in conditions no onboarding retreat can simulate.
- Integrating edge-walkers into the loop isn't charity. It's leverage.
- **Wisdom × Open Tools = Natural Acceleration.** Wisdom squared.
